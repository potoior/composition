# A Comprehensive Template for Graduate English Writing Exam (Part II)

## Main Template Text

**Dear 【Recipient's Identity】,**

**I am 【Your Identity】. I am writing this letter with the primary purpose of 【Purpose of the Letter】 concerning the issue of 【Core Topic】.**

**I would be exceedingly grateful if you could spare a few moments to consider the following elaborations, which are aimed at providing a comprehensive perspective on this matter.**

**First and foremost, it would be a highly judicious move to 【Suggestion/Point 1】. This initiative is not only conducive to 【Benefit 1】 but also plays an indispensable role in 【Benefit 2】.**

**Furthermore, I am firmly convinced that considerable benefits could be derived from 【Suggestion/Point 2】. Such a measure, if implemented, would undoubtedly lead to a significant improvement in 【Specific Outcome】, thereby creating a more harmonious and productive environment for all parties involved.**

**Last but certainly not least, I would like to emphatically recommend 【Suggestion/Point 3】. This proposal serves as a crucial cornerstone for 【Long-term Benefit】 in the foreseeable future, as it allows for timely adjustments based on valuable input.**

**I genuinely hope that you will find the aforementioned proposals insightful and worthy of implementation. Thank you very much for your time and consideration. I am eagerly anticipating your positive response at your earliest convenience.**

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

---

## Instructions for Completion

Simply replace the content within 【】 brackets as follows:

- `【Recipient's Identity】`: Recipient's identity (e.g.: Sir or Madam, Professor Smith, international students)
- `【Your Identity】`: Your identity (e.g.: a senior student, the president of Student Union)
- `【Purpose of the Letter】`: Purpose of writing the letter (e.g.: putting forward constructive suggestions, extending a cordial invitation)
- `【Core Topic】`: Core topic (e.g.: improving campus facilities, the upcoming cultural festival)
- `【Suggestion/Point 1】`: First suggestion/key point
- `【Benefit 1】`: First benefit
- `【Benefit 2】`: Second benefit
- `【Suggestion/Point 2】`: Second suggestion/key point
- `【Specific Outcome】`: Specific outcome
- `【Suggestion/Point 3】`: Third suggestion/key point
- `【Long-term Benefit】`: Long-term benefit

# Universal Template for Graduate English Writing Exam (Part II - Essay)

## Paragraph 1: Chart Description

### First Sentence
The information in the graph clearly shows (topic)

Example: The information in the graph clearly shows the different proportions of residents' consumption in a city of China during the Spring Festival

### Second Sentence
Static chart:
X accounts for the highest percentage at x% of key term, followed by B at y% and C, z%. At the bottom of the chart, k% is regarded as D.

Example: The number of museums dramatically increased/steeply dropped from 4165/4692 in 2013 to 4692/4165 in 2015

Dynamic chart:
There be + noun phrase + in + data heading + data change (static sentence structure)

Example: There was a dramatic increase/steep drop in the number of museums from 4165//4692 in 2013 to 4692/4165 in 2015.

### Third Sentence
The graph, at the first glimpse, seems to be simple, but the deep meaning behind it is worth pondering.

## Paragraph 2: Cause Analysis

### First Sentence
The contributing factors responsible for this phenomenon can be summarized as follows.

### Second Sentence
It is generally recognized that nothing is more important than (four universal reasons) for (key term)

Example 1: It is generally recognized that nothing is more important than useful/ extensive/ good experience (key term).

### Third Sentence
Depending upon/Based upon a rough estimate of the relevant department, nearly ? of the respondents hold the same view.

### Fourth Sentence
(Four universal reasons) exerts (a/an) positive / profound / far-reaching / undesirable influence / effect / impact / impression on (key term)

Example 1: Efficient learning / working exerts positive influence on (key term).

## Paragraph 3: Conclusion and Outlook

### First Sentence
The above-mentioned facts can naturally lead us to the conclusion that great importance should be attached to (key term)

### Second Sentence
Newspapers and news media should promote publicity of it / launch promotions of it/reveal and prevent it.

### Third Sentence
If these measures can be taken effectively, a bright future must be on the way.
